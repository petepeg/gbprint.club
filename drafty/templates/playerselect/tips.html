{% block title %}Printing Tips{% endblock %}

{% endblock %} {% block content %}
  • Print the PDF at 'actual size' or 100%, rather than scaled to fit.
  • Set your margins to the lowest values possible. Depending on your printer some of the guides on the edges will get cropped, but this will ensure the correct size.
  • Use an exacto knife and a metal ruler to get strait cuts, score the ceter line of each card for an easy fold.
  • Once you start cutting you will lose guide lines.
  • You can use a pencil and a ruler to extend the guide lines. I like to just draw the horizonal lines than do all my vertical cuts first.
  • Wrap the printed card around any spare cardstock cards you may have and sleeve them. MTG land cards, playing cards, or old Guild Ball cards work well.
